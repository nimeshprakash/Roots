FamilyTree Connect - User Data Deletion Policy

What Data We Collect

When you use FamilyTree Connect, we access the following data from Facebook:

  • Your name
  • Profile picture
  • Email address (if provided)
  • Facebook User ID
  • Friends list (if you grant permission)

How Your Data is Stored

FamilyTree Connect stores all your family tree data locally in your browser using localStorage. We do NOT store your data on any external servers.

Important: Because data is stored locally in your browser, you have full control and can delete it at any time.

How to Delete Your Data

Option 1: Delete Within the App

1
Log into FamilyTree Connect

Visit the app and sign in with your Facebook account

2
Click the Logout Button

This will disconnect your Facebook account and clear your session

3
Clear Browser Data (Optional)

For complete deletion, clear your browser's localStorage:

  • Chrome/Edge: Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Clear browsing data ‚Üí Check "Cookies and site data"
  • Firefox: Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Clear Data ‚Üí Check "Cookies and Site Data"
  • Safari: Preferences ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Manage Website Data ‚Üí Remove

Option 2: Revoke App Access from Facebook

1
Go to Facebook Settings

Visit facebook.com/settings?tab=applications

2
Find FamilyTree Connect

Look for our app in the list of active apps

3
Remove the App

Click "Remove" to revoke all permissions and disconnect the app from your Facebook account

Option 3: Request Manual Deletion

1
Contact Us

Send an email to our support team (see below)

2
Provide Your Facebook User ID

Include your Facebook User ID so we can verify and process your request

3
Confirmation

We will confirm deletion within 7 business days

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your data, the following information will be removed:

  • All family tree data you created
  • Your Facebook login information
  • Any locally stored preferences
  • All connections to Facebook friends you added

‚ö†Ô∏è Note:

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure to export your family tree data before deleting if you want to keep a copy.

Timeline

Data deletion is processed as follows:

  • Immediate: Local browser data deletion (Options 1 & 2)
  • Within 7 days: Manual deletion requests (Option 3)
  • Within 30 days: Any backup or cached data
